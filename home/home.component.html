OrgBook GC is a searchable public directory of open verifiable data about organizations legally registered in Canada.

home.about-title

This new service uses cutting-edge blockchain-based technology, which provides the foundation to help GC move to a digital economy.

OrgBook GC supports our goal to streamline and modernize services for small and medium sized enterprises (SME) as part of our broader Digital Government Strategy.

Right now, you can use OrgBook GC to quickly verify if an organization is legally registered to do business in Canada as a corporation. You can also find "Doing Business as" names registered by corporations.

In the future, verifiable data about other organizations types such as sole proprietors and societies will be added. As well, other important verifable data such as the permits, licenses, and other accreditations these organizations hold will be added to OrgBook GC.
